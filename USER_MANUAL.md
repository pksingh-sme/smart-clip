## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the SmartClip homepage
2. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
3. Fill in the required information:
   - Username (must be unique)
   - Email address (must be valid)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Check your email for a verification message
6. Click the verification link in the email to activate your account

### Logging In

1. Visit the SmartClip homepage
2. Click the "Log In" button in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. If you have two-factor authentication enabled, enter the code sent to your phone

### Navigating the Interface

The SmartClip interface is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly:

- **Header**: Contains the logo, search bar, and user menu
- **Navigation Menu**: Sidebar with links to Home, Trending, Subscriptions, and Library
- **Main Content Area**: Displays videos, recommendations, and search results
- **Footer**: Contains links to About, Terms, Privacy, and Help

## Video Management

### Uploading Videos

1. Click the "Create" button (camera icon) in the header
2. Select "Upload Video"
3. Choose a video file from your device
4. Add a thumbnail image (optional)
5. Enter a title and description
6. Set privacy options (Public, Private, Unlisted)
7. Add tags to help with discoverability
8. Click "Publish"

**Tips for Uploading:**
- Videos should be in MP4, MOV, or AVI format
- Maximum file size is 10GB
- Recommended resolution is 1080p or higher
- Videos will be processed and available shortly after upload

### Managing Your Videos

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click on the "Videos" tab
3. Here you can:
   - View all your uploaded videos
   - Edit video details (title, description, tags)
   - Change privacy settings
   - Delete videos
   - See view counts and engagement metrics

### Watching Videos

1. Browse or search for videos
2. Click on any video thumbnail to start watching
3. Use the player controls to:
   - Play/Pause
   - Adjust volume
   - Change quality
   - Enter fullscreen mode
   - Access playback speed settings

**Player Features:**
- Adaptive streaming for optimal quality
- Keyboard shortcuts for navigation
- Picture-in-picture mode
- Save to watch later

## Social Features

### Comments

1. While watching a video, scroll down to the comments section
2. Type your comment in the text box
3. Click "Comment" to post
4. To reply to a comment, click "Reply" under the comment
5. To edit or delete your comment, click the three dots next to your comment

**Comment Guidelines:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- No hate speech or personal attacks
- Stay on topic
- Report inappropriate comments

### Likes and Dislikes

1. Below each video, you'll see like and dislike buttons
2. Click the thumbs up to like a video
3. Click the thumbs down to dislike a video
4. Your likes and dislikes help improve recommendations
5. You can change your vote at any time by clicking the opposite button

### Subscriptions

1. To subscribe to a channel, visit the creator's profile
2. Click the "Subscribe" button
3. You'll receive notifications when they upload new content
4. To manage subscriptions, go to your profile and click "Subscriptions"
5. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Subscribed" button

## Personalization

### Recommendations

SmartClip uses AI to provide personalized video recommendations:

- **Home Page**: Videos based on your viewing history and subscriptions
- **Trending**: Popular videos across the platform
- **Recommended**: Videos similar to ones you've watched
- **Continue Watching**: Videos you've started but not finished

**Improving Recommendations:**
- Watch more videos in your preferred categories
- Like videos you enjoy
- Subscribe to channels you follow

### Language Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Settings"
3. Choose "Language" from the left menu
4. Select your preferred language from the dropdown
5. Click "Save Changes"

**Supported Languages:**
- English
- Spanish
- French
- German
- Japanese
- More languages coming soon!

### Theme Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Settings"
3. Choose "Appearance" from the left menu
4. Select your preferred theme:
   - Light Mode (default)
   - Dark Mode
5. Click "Save Changes"

## Profile Management

### Editing Your Profile

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Your Profile"
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Update your:
   - Profile picture
   - Banner image
   - Bio/description
   - Social links
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Privacy Settings

1. Click your profile icon in the top right
2. Select "Settings"
3. Choose "Privacy" from the left menu
4. Adjust settings for:
   - Who can see your videos
   - Who can comment on your videos
   - Who can subscribe to you
   - Activity status visibility
5. Click "Save Changes"

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Video Won't Play**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser

**Upload Failed**
- Check file format and size
- Ensure stable internet connection
- Try uploading at a different time
- Contact support if the issue persists

**Can't Log In**
- Check your email and password
- Reset your password if needed
- Ensure your account is verified
- Check if your account has been suspended

**Poor Video Quality**
- Check your internet speed
- Lower the video quality in player settings
- Close other bandwidth-intensive applications

### Contact Support

If you're experiencing issues not covered in this manual:

1. Visit the Help Center at `/help`
2. Search the knowledge base for solutions
3. Submit a support ticket with:
   - Description of the issue
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Screenshots if applicable
   - Your account email

You can also contact our support team directly at pksingh.sme@gmail.com.

## Account Security

To keep your account secure:

1. Use a strong, unique password
2. Enable two-factor authentication
3. Don't share your login credentials
4. Log out on shared devices
5. Regularly review account activity

If you suspect unauthorized access:
1. Change your password immediately
2. Enable two-factor authentication if not already enabled
3. Contact support at pksingh.sme@gmail.com
