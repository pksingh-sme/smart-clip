## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the StreamHub website
2. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
3. Fill in the required information:
   - Username
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Confirm password
4. Click "Sign Up"
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard

### Logging In

1. Visit the StreamHub website
2. Click on the "Login" button in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. You will be redirected to your dashboard

### Navigating the Interface

The StreamHub interface consists of:

- **Navigation Bar**: At the top of the page with links to main sections
- **Main Content Area**: Where videos, search results, and other content are displayed
- **Sidebar**: (On some pages) Additional navigation and filters
- **Footer**: Links to help, terms, and other information

## Video Management

### Uploading Videos

1. Click on the "Upload" button in the navigation bar or on your dashboard
2. Select a video file from your device
3. Add a thumbnail (optional)
4. Enter a title for your video
5. Add a description
6. Set the visibility (Public, Unlisted, Private)
7. Click "Upload Video"
8. Your video will be processed and will appear on your channel once ready

### Editing Videos

1. Go to your dashboard or profile page
2. Find the video you want to edit
3. Click on the "Edit" button below the video
4. Make your changes to the title, description, or visibility
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Deleting Videos

1. Go to your dashboard or profile page
2. Find the video you want to delete
3. Click on the "Delete" button below the video
4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

## Social Features

### Liking and Disliking

1. Find a video you want to rate
2. Click the thumbs up button to like the video
3. Click the thumbs down button to dislike the video
4. Your rating will be saved and displayed

### Commenting

1. Find a video you want to comment on
2. Scroll to the comments section below the video
3. Type your comment in the text box
4. Click "Comment" to post your comment
5. To reply to a comment, click "Reply" below that comment

### Subscriptions

1. Find a channel you want to subscribe to
2. Click the "Subscribe" button on their profile page or below their videos
3. To unsubscribe, click the "Subscribed" button

## Profile Management

### Editing Profile

1. Click on your profile picture in the navigation bar
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Click "Edit Profile"
4. Make changes to your username, email, or bio
5. Click "Save Changes"

### Viewing Your Dashboard

1. Click on your profile picture in the navigation bar
2. Select "Dashboard" from the dropdown menu
3. Your dashboard shows:
   - Your videos
   - View statistics
   - Subscriber count
   - Recent activity

## Search and Discovery

### Searching for Videos

1. Use the search bar at the top of the page
2. Type your search query
3. Press Enter or click the search icon
4. Browse through the search results

### Recommendations

StreamHub provides personalized video recommendations based on:
- Your viewing history
- Videos you've liked
- Channels you've subscribed to
- Popular videos on the platform

Recommendations appear on your homepage and in the "Recommended" section.

## Multi-language Support

StreamHub supports multiple languages for the user interface:

1. Click on your profile picture in the navigation bar
2. Select "Settings" from the dropdown menu
3. Choose your preferred language from the "Language" dropdown
4. The interface will update to your selected language

Supported languages include:
- English
- Spanish
- French
- German
- Japanese
- And more

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Video won't upload**
   - Check file format (MP4, MOV, AVI supported)
   - Check file size (up to 10GB)
   - Ensure stable internet connection

2. **Can't log in**
   - Check email and password
   - Reset password if needed
   - Contact support if issues persist

3. **Video not playing**
   - Refresh the page
   - Check internet connection
   - Try a different browser

### Contact Support

If you encounter any issues not covered in this manual, please contact our support team at support@streamhub.com.
